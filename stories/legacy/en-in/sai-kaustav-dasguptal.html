Sai Kaustuv Dasgupta, A Graphic Designer With Brittle Bone Disease Makes It To The D-30 Disability Impact List 2021

By KenFolios Editorial
Despite living with a rare brittle bone disease and severe physical disability, Sai Kaustuv Dasgupta has emerged as a graphic designer, singer, author and happiness coach, earning global recognition through the D-30 Disability Impact List 2021.

Andhra Pradesh's Sai Kaustuv Dasgupta has proved that disability is a matter of perception. An author, singer and happiness coach, the 29-year-old was chosen for the D-30 Disability Impact List for 2021, becoming a source of inspiration for people across India and beyond.

Dasgupta was just three years old when he was diagnosed with osteogenesis imperfecta, commonly known as brittle bone disease. The condition weakened his bones significantly and resulted in a disability level of around ninety per cent.

“My parents never made me realise that I am less than other people.”

Born in Siliguri, West Bengal, he experienced several difficult years of depression while growing up. However, constant encouragement from his parents helped him build confidence and pursue his ambitions.

According to Dasgupta, his family always treated him as someone capable of achieving extraordinary things. Their support became the foundation upon which he built his career and identity.

With a strong interest in visual design, he began learning graphic design using his only functioning finger. Over time, his skills improved dramatically and he started receiving projects from clients across India and overseas.

Today, he has worked with international organisations and built a professional reputation that extends far beyond geographical boundaries.

Beyond design, Dasgupta is also a passionate singer and author. His book My Life, My Love, My Dear Swami, published in 2015, sold thousands of copies and was translated into multiple languages.

The book received appreciation from several notable readers and helped spread his message of resilience and positivity.

Sai Kaustuv Dasgupta receiving recognition for his achievements.

With more than 140 songs to his credit, he has also earned recognition as a singer. Over the years he received training in Hindustani classical music and won awards that highlighted both his talent and determination.

Many people know him as a happiness coach. Through his sessions, he guides differently-abled individuals and professionals toward a more positive and fulfilling outlook on life.

What makes his work unique is that he often offers guidance without charging fees, believing that helping others discover happiness is reward enough.

Dasgupta is also recognised for his leadership qualities and has frequently been compared with inspirational global figures who transformed perceptions surrounding disability.

In 2021, Diversability included him in its inaugural D-30 Disability Impact List, a global recognition programme honouring disabled leaders who are creating meaningful impact in society.

Selected from hundreds of nominations spanning multiple countries, Dasgupta's inclusion highlighted India's growing representation in conversations around disability inclusion and leadership.

He continues to encourage families of disabled individuals to provide support, compassion and opportunities rather than limitations.

For him, achievements are among the strongest tools for breaking social stigma and changing public perceptions.

Looking ahead, he hopes to compose more music, expand his design work, write additional books and travel extensively to inspire more people around the world.
