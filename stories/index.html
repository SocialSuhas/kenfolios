It Started With A Story

In January 2013, we set out to use the emerging power of social media to tell stories that mattered. What followed was a journey of more than 5,000 stories, over 100 million people reached, and countless moments of recognition—helping many individuals, businesses, and ideas find wider audiences, and in some cases, even overnight fame. Our storytelling journey was eventually paused and later archived following an internal audit. We hope to begin again soon—wiser, stronger, and ready for the next chapter. Until then, all stories remain archived and inaccessible. When we return to publishing, this space will once again become home to both selected stories from our past and the new stories yet to be written.

And Everything That Matters

Entrepreneurship, social impact, innovation, leadership, education, culture, community building, and much more. The genres above represent only a small part of our storytelling journey.


Over the years, KenFolios featured people, businesses, organizations, ideas, movements, causes, achievements, breakthroughs and moments from virtually every field and walk of life. If something had the power to inform, inspire, create awareness, spark conversation, celebrate achievement, or leave a positive impact, it belonged in the KenFolios Stories archive.

Archive Notice

Following an internal audit in 2021, the KenFolios Stories archive was placed into preservation mode while we reassessed and restructured our storytelling framework. While all stories currently remain archived and inaccessible, we remain optimistic about returning with a stronger foundation and renewed purpose in the future.
